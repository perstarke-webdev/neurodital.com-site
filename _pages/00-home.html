Genre boundaries between Pop and Metal shatter against the mainstream rocks. Oceana, the singer, shifts from angelic singing to brutal shouts, accompanied by powerful guitar and synthesizer sounds, and intricate bass and drum rhythms. The music transitions into pop-infused dance parts. NeuroTidal breaks norms and conventions of Rock music, taking the audience on a sonic journey into self-discovery. The band draws from various influences, including Jazz, Post Rock, and Progressive Metal, merging them into a unique sound. NeuroTidal’s live shows feature a distinctive atmosphere, enhanced by a well-coordinated light show and blacklight elements, contributing significantly to the creation of a special audiovisual concert experience.
